About Me

I'm Dr. Mohamed Elsawaf, currently a PhD Candidate at the Ming Hsieh Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at USC. My research focuses on RF, microwave, and terahertz circuit and system design.

I am passionate about cutting-edge communication technologies and photonic systems that push the limits of data transmission speeds and integration density.
