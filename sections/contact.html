Research Collaboration

I welcome inquiries about research collaboration in RF, microwave, and THz technologies. My current research focuses on:

  • D-band (110-170 GHz) Circuit Design - RFIC and sub-THz systems
  • Substrate-Integrated Waveguides - Mode-division multiplexing and concurrent dual-mode excitation
  • Plastic and Dielectric Waveguides - Low-dispersion techniques and packaging
  • RF System Modeling - Device modeling and simulation
  • Integrated Passive Components - Miniaturization and optimization

Feel free to reach out if you're interested in collaborative research opportunities or have questions about my published work.
